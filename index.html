What you can do

Everything you need to keep a beautiful cultural journal and share it with friends.

📝

Log activities

Record books, movies, theatre, exhibitions, and concerts with notes, photos, location, and date. Keep some loggs private — just for you.

👥

Follow friends

See what your friends are reading, watching, and visiting in a beautiful social feed. Send follow requests to private accounts.

📷

Add photos

Capture the moment with up to 5 photos per activity — from your camera or library. Crop your profile avatar to look your best.

💬

Comment & like

Interact with your friends' activities. Share your thoughts, see who liked a logg, and get replies to your comments.

📍

Location search

Search and autocomplete locations when logging — or let the app detect your city automatically.

📈

Your profile

Build your cultural profile with stats, filter your loggs by type, and explore your journal with streaks, weekly summaries, and a calendar view.

🔒

Private accounts

Go private so only approved followers can see your loggs. Manage follow requests and control who's in your circle.

🔔

Notifications

Get notified when someone follows you, likes your logg, or comments. Push notifications keep you in the loop even when the app is closed.

📖

Your journal

Track your cultural streak, see weekly summaries, browse a monthly recap by type, and view your loggs on a calendar.

How it works

1

2
